Our Story

Born from
passion.

NOIR was born from a simple conviction: that dining is not just about eating — it's about feeling something. Chef Marco Valdés spent fifteen years in kitchens across Europe before returning home with a singular vision.

Every dish on our menu tells a story. We source directly from local farms, work with the seasons, and let the ingredients speak. No shortcuts. No compromises.

Our dining room holds just 36 guests at a time. Every detail — the lighting, the music, the space between tables — is intentional. You come here to be present.
